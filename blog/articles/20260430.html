11th: G-STATIONERY (@G_cyberdrive)

#あなたが思う一番かっこいいペンを教えてください
STAEDTLER REG Limited Edition Matte Black
シャーペンならこれ。メカニカル感、最高。 pic.twitter.com/fKWKhCDtsG

— G-STATIONERY (@G_cyberdrive) April 26, 2026

G-STATIONERY's pick is STAEDTLER REG, limited color.

There's no disappointment in matte black. That is world's rule. Parker Ingenuity slim, which I picked is also painted in matte black.

The name "REG" comes from regulator, the mechanics to adjust amount of lead dispensed in one knock. Black mechanics... It's pure romance for me.

13th: だいず (@daizu00t7)

#あなたが思う一番かっこいいペンを教えてください

だいず's pick is Pilot S20, mother-of-pearl customized.

Mother-of-pearl inlay; This craft utilizes layers of shell that reflect and interfere with light, creating complex, shifting colors depending on the angle.

It's frustrating that the structural color can't be expressed in photos. I wonder what だいず is seeing.

Not only appearance, but also the base is S20. It's good it can be practical, because I often hear its good review.

siame's pick is Parker Duofold, sterling silver body.

I can tell just by looking at it. It's expensive.

A pristine sterling silver body with gold and black trim. These colors look like neutral, but they actually have a surprisingly strong presence. However, this pen manages them in quite a delicate balance. I'm just feasting my eyes on it.

16th: おうし♉︎(文房具) (@taurustationery)

モンブラン スターウォーカー アーバンスピリット ボールペン #あなたが思う一番かっこいいペンを教えてください pic.twitter.com/ankvbNNNCE

— おうし♉︎(文房具) (@taurustationery) April 26, 2026

おうし's pick is Montblanc Starwalker.

Black body with silver trim is classic combination. It’s less flashy and has a calmer, smarter ambience compared to the classic black-and-gold, JPS color.

Montblanc's icon is "White Star" on top, but this model has clear resin over it. The picture took from top is in reply tree, so please visit post if you interested.

17th: ケンタゴン (@chdocobay)

#あなたが思う一番かっこいいペンを教えてください

セーラー万年筆
CYLINTパティナ

ワケわかんないくらいカッコいいです！ https://t.co/cv9QyCHjwx pic.twitter.com/mh6ZH1Pexy

— ケンタゴン (@chdocobay) April 26, 2026

ケンタゴン's pick is Sailor CYLINT Patina.

The pattern called as "Brown Patina". I've seen Nukayaki coloring customized body pen in picture before, but what a clear and beautiful mottled pattern.

I used to think that techniques with hard-to-control individual variations were a poor fit for large corporations that prioritize consistent quality. However, they beautifully embrace these variations, tying everything together perfectly with the trim. The craftsmanship of Sailor is truly magnificent.

In addition, This will be aged with this texture. I found an indication of the rabbit hole...!

甘味's pick is MontBlanc #1468.

Sterling silver, again.

Based on iconic MontBlanc Meisterstück stripe pattern, made of Sterling silver.

Less black parts than Duofold before, it looked to allowing more luxury, gorgeous ambience.

18K bicolor nib also shining, and I feel it would be a waste to use it, so it's likely to just end up in my drawer...

19th: 榊/さかき (@sakaki_bungu)

#あなたが思う一番かっこいいペンを教えてください pic.twitter.com/F0fARcEBea

— 榊/さかき (@sakaki_bungu) April 26, 2026

榊's pick is TWSBI Precision.

The layout, angle the photo are taken is so cool. I want to learn how to use the keyboard, ink bottles, and other small items...

It's hard to see because of lights, this is silver body. Slim full-metal body, the machining precision looks incredibly high.

Hexagonal body and cylindrical grip - common design such as Faber-Castell TK-FINE Vario L and uni Jetstream edge.

However, is there any Pros? I think hexagonal grip is what I exactly wanted, so this design feels a bit teasing to me.

文ある's pick is LAMY Dialog 3.

LAMY is common for functional and beautiful products, meticulously designed by exclusive designer one by one.

One of these, Dialog designed by who designed pico which is cute, rounded body.

Unlike this cute body, there is the incredible gimmick of a retractable pen tip inside. The gap between its cute look and the high-tech interior is absolutely irresistible.
